## My Personal Recommendation

**Choose LinguaMorph** because:
1. **Professional yet approachable** - serious learners will respect it
2. **Memorable** - good sound, easy to spell
3. **Describes the USP** - the morphological parsing is your differentiator
4. **International** - works in any language, room to grow
5. **Domain available** - linguamorph.com is free
6. **No cultural baggage** - won't alienate Estonian or Finnish learners

**Tagline:** "Master Finnish & Estonian through intelligent morphological analysis"

## Final Thought

**"FinEstDB"** works if you want to keep it simple and descriptive. It's not *bad*, just functional.

But if you want something that:
- **Stands out** in the language learning space
- **Communicates sophistication**
- **Scales beyond just FI/ET**
- **Is memorable and marketable**

Then **LinguaMorph**, **KieliPath**, or **LemmaLoop** would be much stronger choices.
